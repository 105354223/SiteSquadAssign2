IT Security Analyst

Reference Number:GDS01
Position Title:IT Security Analyst
Salary Range:AUD 85,000 – 95,000 per annum + superannuation
Employment Type:Full-time, ongoing
Location:Melbourne CBD (with hybrid work options)
Reporting Line:Reports to Senior Security Manager, Cybersecurity Division

About the Role

The IT Security Analyst is responsible for safeguarding government systems, data,
and digital services from evolving cyber threats. You will play a critical role in
monitoring security alerts, identifying vulnerabilities, and implementing solutions to
ensure compliance with security standards. Working within the Cybersecurity Division,
you will help build a secure, resilient digital environment for citizens and government staff.

Key Responsibility

  • Monitor, detect, and respond to security incidents across government IT infrastructure
  • Perform vulnerability assessments and penetration testing on networks and applications
  • Maintain and update security tools, including firewalls, IDS/IPS, and SIEM platforms
  • Investigate breaches, document findings, and provide recommendations for remediation
  • Support the development and enforcement of cybersecurity policies and compliance standards
  • Collaborate with IT teams to ensure systems are patched and secured

Eseential Requiremnet

  1. Bachelor’s degree in Cybersecurity, Information Technology, or related field
  2. 2–4 years of professional experience in IT security or network administration
  3. Hands-on knowledge of firewalls, intrusion detection systems, and SIEM tools
  4. Knowledge of ISO 27001, NIST, or other security frameworks
  5. Strong understanding of risk management and incident response processes
  6. Strong communication skills to explain security issues to non-technical staff

Preferable Requirements

  1. Industry certifications such as CISSP, CISM, CEH, or CompTIA Security+
  2. Experience working in government or large enterprise environments
  3. Familiarity with cloud security (AWS, Azure, or GCP)
  4. Knowledge of endpoint detection and response (EDR) tools
  5. Experience leading security audits or compliance reviews

Why Join Us?

  • Play a key role in protecting critical national digital infrastructure
  • Work in a collaborative environment with opportunities for professional growth
  • Access to cutting-edge security tools and training
  • Flexible hybrid working arrangements and career development pathways
